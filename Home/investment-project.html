MSMEs having land are eligible to obtain Certificate of In-principle Approval based on self-certification which will be valid for 3 years and 6 months for clearances given below. The enterprise is required to apply for applicable regular approvals not later than 3 years from the date of issue of the Certificate of In-principle Approval.
